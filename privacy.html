Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to me. This policy explains what data I collect, why I collect it, and how I protect and use it.

01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Saurav “dearlipu” Panda (referred to as “I”, “me”, or “dearlipu”) collects, uses, and discloses personal information when you visit dearlipu websites, contact me, request services (NextGen), or interact with museum-related content (OdiArt).

By using my website or services you accept the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the services and contact me to discuss alternatives.

02

Information I Collect

I collect information that helps me provide better services and communicate with you. This includes:

Personal Information

Information you give me directly when you contact, book a service, or sign up for communications:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone)
  • Service details and device problem descriptions
  • Billing or address information if required for a service

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit the site I may collect technical data automatically:

  • Browser and device information
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages visited, time on site, and referral source (analytics)
03

How I Use Your Information

I use data to operate and improve services, deliver support, and communicate with you. Typical uses include:

Responding to inquiries and service requests
Providing, maintaining and improving services
Processing payments and invoices when applicable
Sending occasional updates, offers, or notifications (with opt-out)
Analyzing site usage to improve the website
Protecting the site from abuse and security threats
04

Information Sharing and Disclosure

I will not sell your personal information. I only share data in limited cases:

With Your Consent

I may share information when you ask me to (for example, with a shipping provider or a partner for a specific project).

Service Providers

I may use trusted third-party services (hosting, payment processors, analytics) who process information on my behalf. They are contractually required to protect your data.

Legal & Safety

I may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property, or to respond to lawful requests by public authorities.

05

Data Security

I take reasonable steps to protect your information. Technical and organizational measures include:

Encryption & HTTPS

Site traffic is encrypted via HTTPS. Sensitive data is handled securely when processed by providers.

Access Control

Access to personal data is limited to those who need it to perform services or support.

Backups & Protection

Backups and regular security maintenance are applied where appropriate to reduce risk.

Please note: no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure — if you have concerns about security, contact me using the details below.

Access

Request a copy of personal information I hold about you.

Correction

Ask to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.

Deletion

Object & Restrict

Object to or request restriction of certain processing activities.

To exercise any rights, contact me at the email below. I will respond within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made I will update the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, notify you. Please review this page periodically.
